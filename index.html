We strive to bring awareness to air pollution and quality in St.Lucia. Clean air means better health. Help us keep our nation's air clean and clear. We organize recyclying, composting and bringng awareness one community at a time.

Why we shouldn't burn waste

Burning waste like tree branches and other forms of garbage releases toxic fumes into the atmosphere such as carbon dioxide (Co2) and nitrogen . These are called green house gases. And they're a reason.
These gases usually would be able to escape our atmosphere, but there is wayyy to much now! From factories, cars and burning of trash. That's just some sources. The trees cannot absorb so much co2 and it can't escape so it stays here in our atmosphere and creates that 'greenhouse' effect.
